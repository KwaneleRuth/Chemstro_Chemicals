Services

We supply low-pressure and high-pressure solar geysers

A solar geyser offers effective cost reductions on energy consumption to heat water for bathing and washing. we at Chemstro offer you affordable but high-quality solar, electric and gas geyser products without any hustle. Solar geysers depend on the basic principle of using the suns energy to heat water. As a result, it has been that by using one at home, water heating costs should decrease by 50%–80%. This is a massive saving for the average house hold. Also, its free energy so you’re safe if ZESA decides to hike up the electricity fees or insist on more load shedding.

Coolness without the breeze

We have a large range of air conditioners at competitive prices, and provide expert installation, support and maintenance. We service both homes and businesses. We offer a carefully selected range of premium air conditioners, for domestic and commercial spaces. For independent control of air quality in individual rooms, our wall-mounted air conditioners provide optimum air cooling and heating, with energy-saving inverter technology.

Our ceiling-mounted four-way cassette air conditioners, also with inverter technology, are ideal for air conditioning in larger spaces, in upmarket domestic or commercial environments. Alternatively, gain uniform control over air quality throughout your home, business or retail space with our ducted air conditioning systems, ideal for cooling or heating multiple rooms.

Get your water tested

We supply, distribute and install water purifiers or filters for borehole, municipal and rain water. Water purifiers, depending on the type, remove hardness, lime, bacteria, and other organic and inorganic particles or contaminants found water supplies. Purifying your water means no more stomach bugs and unforeseen illnesses you will also get that freshwater taste and smell. Most of these purifiers are easy to install and we offer online assistance for all questions and problems that might occur with the installation of a purifier! You will only know what is in your water if you purify it. Hope you have pure healthy water in your home!
